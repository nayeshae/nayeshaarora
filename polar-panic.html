01. Overview

Polar Panic is a 2–4 player multiplayer tabletop board game that integrates environmental stakes into core gameplay mechanics. By utilizing a progressive board-building system and resource management, the game forces players to navigate a shifting, unpredictable Arctic landscape where strategy must adapt as quickly as the ice melts.
